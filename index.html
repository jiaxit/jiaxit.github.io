Short Bio

I am now a software engineer at Google. I obtain my Ph.D. from School of Computing Science, Simon Fraser University, advised by Prof. Ke Wang. My research interests focus on data mining and applied machine learning with special interest on building intelligent recommendation system. Before joining Simon Fraser University, I obtained my B.Eng. in Software Engineering from Wuhan University.
